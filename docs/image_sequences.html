12. Image SequencesÔÉÅ

Make a video out of 1 frame: ffmpeg -loop 1 -i image.png -c:v libx264 -t 15 -pix_fmt yuv420p -vf scale=320:240 out.mp4 Set -t to however many seconds you want the clip to be. Set scale to the dimensions of the video it needs to match.
